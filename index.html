OUR STORY

From a 700 sq ft garage to Grand Rapids' favorite taproom

Born from backyard homebrewing and a shared sense of adventure, Arvon Brewing Co. started in 2016 when Jacob paired his passion for bold flavors with Brett's business know-how. What began as a family dream took shape in a tiny 700 square foot production space in Grandville, Michigan ‚Äî no taproom, no frills, just great beer.

For 18 months, Arvon brewed and canned out of that small space, shipping beer to stores across Metro Detroit and building a loyal following one hazy IPA at a time. Word spread. Shelves cleared. The buzz was real.

In February 2020, they opened the doors to their Grand Rapids taproom ‚Äî a beautifully converted 1948 Oldsmobile dealership on Division Avenue. The space already had the bones of a brewery (floor drains, plumbing, heavy-duty electrical), and the team turned it into something special: a modern, welcoming space where the beer speaks for itself.

Today, Arvon releases 4-5 fresh batches every week, from their celebrated New England IPAs and smoothie-thick fruited sours to a barrel-aged stout program that's earned a reputation across the state. Every beer is crafted to excite ‚Äî and every visit feels like coming home.
